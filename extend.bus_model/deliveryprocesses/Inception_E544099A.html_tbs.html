Этап: Inception
The goal of the Inception phase is to achieve concurrence among all stakeholders on the lifecycle objectives for the project. In a project which focuses only on business modeling, the significance of this phase depends on the degree of change the business has to go through. During this phase the main objectives and expectations are set for the business-modeling effort and the appropriate business-modeling scenario is chosen for the project.
