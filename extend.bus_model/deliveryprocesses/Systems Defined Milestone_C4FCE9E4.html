Веха: Systems Defined Milestone
At the end of the Transition phase, all the systems needed for the technical solution are specified, in terms of Vision, Use-case and Analysis Models.
Основное описание

Evaluation Criteria:

  • The Visions for the automated systems which will be developed or acquired are specified.
  • A preliminary set of Use-case and Analysis models are developed for each system and reviewed.
  • Any changes to the Business Analysis/Design Models due to technical constraints are documented and reviewed.

Main Artifacts:  

  • Business Analysis/Design Models – updated and reviewed by all stakeholders.
  • System Vision (for each system) – stable and reviewed by all stakeholders.
  • System Use-case and Analysis Models - preliminary.
