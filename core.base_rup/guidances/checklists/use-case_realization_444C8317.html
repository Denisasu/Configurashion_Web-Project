Check the Consistency between the Use-Case Realizations and the User-Experience Storyboards

The User-Experience Model is the contract between the presentation logic development teams and the business logic development teams.  Thus, it is important that the presentation requirements and the business aspects of the system are synchronized.

Every Use-Case Realization that interacts with a human Actor should have an associated User-Experience Storyboard. The associated flows of events and flow diagrams should be consistent.
