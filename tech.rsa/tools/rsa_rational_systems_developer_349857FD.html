Основное описание
IBM® Rational® Systems Developer is an integrated design and construction product built on top of Eclipse. It leverages all of the power of Eclipse and includes plug-ins that enable software architects and model-driven developers to create well-architected C++ and Javaä J2SE applications leveraging Version 2 of the Unified Modeling Language (UML).
