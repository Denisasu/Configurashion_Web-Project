Content

This Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) plug-in builds on top of Classic Rational Unified Process (RUP), adding guidance for the Software Architect and Designer in developing a Service Model.  A Service Model is a model representing a portfolio of services, that can be used as the basis for implementation activities already in RUP. 

This plug-in also provides the connection between business modeling and service modeling.  Many SOA projects use business process models to understand the business, functional requirements and the services required to support a process. For more information on this plug-in, see the Whitepaper: The RUP Update for Service-Oriented Architecture.
